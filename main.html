About The Conference

It is a fact that the world has been witnessing a fundamental shift for balancing growing energy demands with that of environment protection. With energy sustainability at the heart of Vision 2030, it is no wonder that the Kingdom has committed itself to reach Net Zero emissions by 2060, placing it at the forefront of the fight against climate change. Johnson Controls International, JCI, a global leader for smart, healthy and sustainable buildings is kicking off Innovation for a Sustainable Tomorrow event, highlighting the critical role of energy sustainability as it relates to buildings efficiency and environmental protection.
