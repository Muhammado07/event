About Innovation Conference

The Innovation Conference will feature government officials and leading figures from the industrial sectors in specialized panel discussions and seminars to shed light on the latest innovative trends and updates on the road towards achieving the Kingdom’s sustainability goals, in light with Saudi Arabia’s pledge to cut its carbon emissions to net zero by 2060, in addition to the distinguished projects and initiatives that are launched to diversify energy sources to increase dependency on renewable energy to 50% by 2030.
